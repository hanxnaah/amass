Tell stories that matter

Amass Media aims to empower organizations to tell their social impact stories by creating an online platform that connects nonprofits, social ventures, and small businesses with talented filmmakers looking for interesting projects to work on.

Last year, Amass Media won funding from the Big Ideas @ Berkeley Competition to launch the idea. We are currently matching projects informally before the official site launches soon.
